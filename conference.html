About the Conference

The inaugural Indiana Quant Conference represents a groundbreaking initiative to foster collaboration between academic researchers and industry practitioners in quantitative finance and economic machine learning.

This one-day intensive event brings together leading minds from hedge funds, faculty members from the Kelly School of Business, and students across all levels - from undergraduate economics and finance clubs to Master's and Ph.D. programs. Our goal is to create meaningful dialogue about the latest developments in quantitative methods, machine learning applications in finance, and the evolving landscape of economic research.
